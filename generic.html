Biography

A Good story needs a good storyteller.

Clearance Mogau Mmako AKA Lord'Verb [Founder & Director] of 7vD, has a story to tell to ears for human hope. A South African Versatile DJ, Producer, Album Compiler, Podcaster & Event strategiest. Born & bred in the roots of Limpopo Zebediela small-town village Ga-Molapo. Lord'Verb Has started Listening to House Music arround 2008/9 and Started Deejaying 2011 withn influence of Dj Kent,Dj Sleek T, Vinny Da Vincy and few more. Just Arround 2021 Has medged with DJ Miza for monthly residency at his show Golden cirfcle at TransAfrica Radio 872.Fast-Forward to 2021 Also was contracted to be a Album-Compiler at Tapedeck-produkxions pty(ltd) which are Titled Soul'Jar.
